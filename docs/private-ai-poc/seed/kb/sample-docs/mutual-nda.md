This is a sample document used to demonstrate aiAdaptiv's document intelligence.

## Key terms
- Governing law: Germany; exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Munich.
- Liquidated damages for breach: EUR 100,000 per breach, without prejudice to injunctive relief.
- Return-or-destroy obligation: within 14 business days of a written request.
- Carve-outs: information that is public, independently developed, or already lawfully known is not confidential.
