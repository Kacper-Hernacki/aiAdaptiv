# Master Services Agreement — SAMPLE (for demo)

This is a sample document used to demonstrate aiAdaptiv's document intelligence.

- Provider: Northstar Consulting Ltd
- Client: Meridian Retail GmbH
- Services: IT consulting and managed support
- Effective date: 1 March 2026

## Commercial terms
- Fees: EUR 12,000 per month
- Payment terms: net 30 days from invoice
- Initial term: 24 months
- Renewal: automatically renews for successive 12-month terms unless either party gives 90 days written notice before the end of the current term.

## Liability & termination
- Provider's liability under this agreement is unlimited.
- The Client may terminate only for material breach not cured within 30 days.
- The Provider may terminate for convenience on 30 days written notice.

## Other
- Governing law: Germany; courts of Frankfurt.
- Confidential information must be returned or destroyed within 14 days of termination.
